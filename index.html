Q&A

What is Weady AI?

Weady AI is a website builder that uses artificial intelligence to create stunning one-page websites with all the features you need.

How does Weady AI work?

Weady AI uses artificial intelligence technology to analyze your business needs and preferences and then generates a website design that perfectly matches your brand.

Do I need any coding skills to use Weady AI?

No coding skills are required to use Weady AI. Weady AI is user-friendly and intuitive, making it easy for anyone to create a beautiful website.

What features can I expect from the Weady AI website builder?

The Weady AI Website Builder includes all the basic features for a single-page website, such as contact forms, social media integration, analytics, and more.

Can I customize my website design with Weady AI?

Yes, you can customize your website design to your liking with Weady AI. You can also choose from a wide range of color schemes to match your brand.

Is Weady AI affordable?

Yes, Weady AI offers affordable pricing plans that are suitable for all types of companies, from startups to large corporations. In addition, there are no hidden fees or charges.

Can I try Weady AI before I buy?

Yes, Weady AI offers a free trial period so you can try out the website builder and see if it's right for you before signing up for a subscription.

What sites can I create on the One page plan?

One page tariff usually provides an opportunity to create a one-page site, which can contain basic information about your business or project. This can be a Landing Page, a personal site, a contact site, and other uses are possible.
